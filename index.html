Event Highlight

Our program features a variety of impactful activities and initiatives designed to educate, inspire, and create lasting change in our communities.

Community Clean-up Drive

“I have been several times to Nigeria, but I must say that I was very impressed about the organisation and turn over of the event. I had an amazing welcome at the Xportia house and the whole team took good care of me. We had excellent attendees that were very interested in studying business. Everyone was asking very good questions. It has been an amazing experience to interact with all the attendees and understand more about what they are looking for. This is quality at its maximum! I am looking forward to coming back and participating at these events with Xportia Global Services” Luis Fernando Aguilar Alvarez (International student representative for EU Business School).

Participants received hands-on education about waste management, recycling practices, and the impact of pollution on local ecosystems. Many volunteers reported that this experience changed their perspective on everyday consumption habits.

Sustainable Living Workshop

In addition, host for the event, Abisola Bamgboye who is the Lead Consultant at Xportia Global Services during her presentation stressed the need for intending students to work with accredited and certified educational consultants while noting her company’s team of experienced professionals and how they have been able to assist hundred of Nigerian students achieve their study abroad dreams over the years.

Attendees participated in hands-on demonstrations of composting, upcycling, and energy conservation techniques. Each participant left with a personalized sustainability action plan and a starter kit containing reusable items to replace single-use plastics.

Youth Environmental Leadership Summit

Our youth summit empowered the next generation of environmental leaders through specialized training, mentorship, and collaborative project development. Students from 25 schools participated in this transformative three-day event.

The summit featured keynote speeches from renowned environmentalists, breakout sessions on advocacy and community organizing, and a project incubator where teams developed proposals for local environmental initiatives. Several projects have already secured funding and are being implemented in communities.
